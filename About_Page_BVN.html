Our Mission & Values

At Bloom Valley Nursery, we believe that every home deserves a touch of nature. As a local, family-owned business, our mission is to provide our community with the highest quality, sustainably grown plants. We are deeply committed to eco-friendly horticulture, offering native plant species that thrive in our local climate while supporting the ecosystem. Whether you are building your first indoor garden or redesigning your entire landscape, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you grow with confidence.




About Our Nursery

Founded in 2011, Bloom Valley Nursery grew out of a deep-rooted passion for bringing vibrant, sustainable plant life into everyday spaces. What started as a small backyard greenhouse has blossomed into a full-service nursery dedicated to eco-conscious horticulture. We specialize in locally sourced native plants, drought-tolerant succulents, and high-quality gardening supplies. Our team of lifelong gardeners is committed to helping you cultivate your perfect green space, whether you're a seasoned botanist or just potting your very first houseplant.
