Community Involvements

We proudly donate seeds and soil to the local community garden, and our team volunteers at the annual city-wide tree planting event every spring. We also partner with local schools to provide educational workshops on sustainable gardening practices.




Events and Collaborations

Spring Planting Workshop: Join us on the first Saturday of May for a FREE workshop on building sustainable garden beds, in collaboration with the local gardening club!

Summer Harvest Festival: Celebrate the bounty of the season with us on August 15th! Enjoy workshops, local vendors, and family-friendly activities.

Fall Garden Cleanup: Get your garden ready for winter with our expert-led cleanup event on October 10th. Bring your tools and we'll provide the mulch!
